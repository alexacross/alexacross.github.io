Background

Christmas is celebrated every year on December 25 in honor of the birth of Christ. During this time of the year, people gather together in His honor and celebrate by enganging in many fun winter activites, such as ice skating, sitting by the fireplace, watching a Christmas movie, or simply just spending time with one another. This holiday is commonly represented with tree lights, presents, and the colors red, white, and green.

Why Christmas?

The best time of the year, and certainly the greatest holiday of the year, is undoubtedly Christmas, or the celebration of the birth of Christ. This festive holidays allows for family-reunions, traditional activities, classic feasts, and ultimate joy and happiness. This period of chestnut-roasting, snowman making, and present wrapping is guarenteed to alleviate any stress or hardship one may be experiencing. To represent their festivity, people decorate their special Christmas tree with colorful lights, shiny tinsel, and garnishing ornaments; they may also embellish the interior and exterior of their houses with items such as candy canes, fake snowmen, santas, and more.
